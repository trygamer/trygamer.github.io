Dodge the Bullets

by: Kaiden

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I decide to create this game because I wanted to go for an endless type of game and this what came up in mind.

2. What I Learned

I learned to make the instructions more detailed and add more text in menu screen.

3. Architecture of My Game

I have a moving heart which gives the player the advantage. The player has hearts that can go down depending on how many missiles hit the player.

4. Technical Implementation

The most particular aspect of the game that I'm most proud of is the bullets that are stuck in the air. It makes the game more unusual than other game it is definition of breaking the law of physics and going above and beyond.

5. Most Challenging Part

The part of my game that I'am most proud of is the moving even though it was one of the hard parts of game it give it a more unique feel to it. I first gave up on the idea after trial and fail endlessly. Then I decide to ask for help and then the coding got easier from their.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I started out player dodging missiles endlessly then I decided to make the missiles go faster every 10 seconds and my favorite part is a heart and when it gets hit it'll give the player an extra life and the heart will also move to a different location.
